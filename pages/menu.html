All Our Dishes and Sauces Are Homemade!

Step 1: Choose Your Meat
  • •

    Al pastor

    Also known as ‘Tacos Al Pastor’ is our spit-grilled pork shoulder marinated using a lightly spiced Adobe marinade. This recipe originates from the Central Mexican region of Puebla and Mexico City. (Not spicy)

  • •

    Biria

    This is a slow cooked beef stew flavoured using Adobe which consists of charred tomatillo, onion, Papillas chillies, dried chillies, garlic, herbs, and spices. This delicious beef dish is cooked in a consommé to enhance its textures and flavours. (Light spicy) (9)

  • •

    Fried Chicken thighs

    Fried grilled chicken which has been marinated in a lemon, pepper and herb marinade. (Not spicy)
