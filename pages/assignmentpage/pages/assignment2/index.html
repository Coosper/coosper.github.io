Welcome to Coosper's Winery!

Where everyone is welcome no matter what your experience level. Our website is for everyone from new beginners to professional winery and vineyard owners.

To learn more about wine making browse this website for in depth articles. Whether you decide to make wine or not: we hope that this website proves to be an important resource in your search for wine making knowledge.

Attempting a recipe or making wine gives you a sense of accomplishment each and every time you try a new recipe or step into the winemaking business, thus we hope that eventually you'll feel inspired to start your own business.

A beginner?

No problem! Our website aims to be friendly toward's complete beginners. We'll take you through the entire process of making your first ever batch of wine.

A home winemaker doesn't need machines or expensive instruments for making wine. Some fragile buckets and measuring cups and spoons are more than enough.

On our website you can find various recipe's fit for beginners. And various things a beginner may need to consider when brewing wine.

A homebrewing veteran?

We'll have plenty to offer you aswell! Brewing wine is an ongoing process and it'll never hurt to brush up on your knowledge of wines.

Seeing as someone with a bit more experience under their belt is more likely to have the fancy equipment required, like various carboys and electric siphons. Our website includes various delicate recipes that'd require such equipment.

As such the winemaking veteran will find plenty of use from our website.
